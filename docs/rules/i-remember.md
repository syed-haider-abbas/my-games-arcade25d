
# I-Remember

Test your memory by finding all the matching pairs!

## How to Play
Tap a card to flip it over. Then tap another one to find its match.

## Rules
*   **Match Mode**: 
    *   **Easy**: Find **2** matching cards.
    *   **Hard**: Find **4** matching cards!
*   **Mistakes**: If the cards don't match, they will flip back over. Remember where they are!
*   **Win**: Clear the entire board to win.

## Pro Tip
**Say it out loud!** Saying the name of the picture when you flip it helps your brain remember where it is.
