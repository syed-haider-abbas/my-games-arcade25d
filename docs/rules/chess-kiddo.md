## Special Moves
*   **Pawn**: Moves forward, but captures diagonally. If it reaches the end, it becomes a Queen!
*   **Check**: When the King is in danger. You must save him!
*   **Checkmate**: The King is trapped. Game Over!

## Pro Tip
**Control the center squares.** Pieces in the middle can go more places than pieces on the side!
