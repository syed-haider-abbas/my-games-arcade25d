## Rules
1.  **Start**: You need to roll a **6** to get an animal out of your base.
2.  **Move**: Roll the dice and move that many steps.
3.  **Capture**: If you land on someone else's animal, you send them back to start! (Unless they are on a Star ‚≠ê)
4.  **Win**: Be the first to get all 4 animals to the center triangle.

## Pro Tip
**Don't go alone!** Try to keep your animals spread out so if one gets sent back, the others are still safe.
