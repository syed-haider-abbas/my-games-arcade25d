
# Sliding Puzzle

Unscramble the picture or numbers by sliding the tiles into the correct order.

## How to Move
You can only move a tile if it is next to the **Empty Space**.

## Rules
*   **Slide**: Tap a tile next to the empty hole to slide it in.
*   **Order**:
    *   **Numbers**: 1, 2, 3... top to bottom.
    *   **Picture**: Make the picture look right!
*   **Hints**: Toggle the "Eye" button to peek at the finished picture.

## Pro Tip
**Solve row by row.** Fix the top row first, then the next one. The last two rows are the trickiest!
