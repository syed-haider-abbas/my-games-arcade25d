## How to Play
*   **Take Turns**: You and your opponent take turns dropping one disc at a time.
*   **Gravity**: The disc always falls to the **bottom** of the column.
*   **Block Them**: If you see your opponent getting 3 in a row, drop your disc to stop them!

## Pro Tip
**Control the Center!** It's easier to make connections if you have pieces in the middle column.
